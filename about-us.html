Our Story

From a small beginning to a nationwide supplier.

Founded in Pune, GreenNets started with a simple mission: to provide India's farmers and industrial workers with reliable, high-quality protective coverings. We saw a need for durable products that could withstand our country's diverse and often harsh climate. What began as a local supplier has grown into a trusted national brand, known for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We are proud to support the backbone of our nation's economy with products that are built to last.

Our Mission & Vision

Our guiding principles.

Our Mission

To empower farmers and industries with superior protective solutions that enhance productivity, ensure safety, and deliver exceptional value, all while maintaining a commitment to sustainable practices.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted and innovative brand for agricultural and industrial protective coverings, recognized for our unwavering quality and dedication to our customers' success.
